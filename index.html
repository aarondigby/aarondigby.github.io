About us

Welcome to Supply Chain. This website is aimed at providing students a general idea of what a supply chain is, how it is used in businesses and how they can make their own.

We hope that this website will help students who are either already studying Business Studies at GCSE level, or students who are thinking about studing but are not sure if this subject is for them. There are many more aspects to a business than Supply Chain and Supply Chain Management, but we have chosen to cover only this section so we do not give to much information.

If you have any questions about something you have seen on this website, or would just like to ask some supply chain questions, please do send us an email to the address at the bottom of the page.

What is a Supply Chain

A supply chain is a network between a company and its suppliers to produce and distribute a specific product or service, and the supply chain represents the steps it takes to get the product or service to the customer. Supply chain management is also a crucial factor for businesses, as an optimized supply chain results in lower costs and a faster production cycle.
